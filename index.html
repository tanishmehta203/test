SAM GEARS INDIA PVT. LTD

Sam Gears is one of the leading manufacturers, and exporters of transmission gears, gears shafts, bull gears, rear axle exporters, differential axle shafts for all types of Tractors like Massey Ferguson Zetor, Ford, Romania UTB 650 . Established in 1996,the company is well adept at executing turnkey projects and has made a distinct identity for itself in its sphere of operation. In the business since 1996, We have vast experience in the competitive field of Gear Production & exports. With a firm commitment to quality and reliable services, we slowly moved ahead. Today we are among the leading companies in our sphere of business. We provide our services to Indian domestic as well as overseas markets.
